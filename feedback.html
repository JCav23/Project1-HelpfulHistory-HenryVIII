Helpful History is a series of educational websites dedicated to helping everybody learn about some of the most famous historical figures.

This first entry into our series examines the life of King Henry VIII, who sat on the English throne from 1509 until his death in 1547.

Here you will find information about details of some of the history he is known for, including his life at Hampton Court Palace, and the marriages to each of his six wives

You will also find a feedback page where you can let us know if we were helpful, share your ideas for any future pages, and vote on who should be the next historical figure in our series
